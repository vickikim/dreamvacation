Paris is the heart of France, the capital city. Paris is known to be the love city, because of its beautiful scenery, its romantic language, popularity as a honeymoon destination. The most well known sight seeing location is a the Eiffel Tower. Tourist experience unforgettable time there by just looking at it, but better, by taking seat at the highest floors of the tower. The view from the top of the towering building is magnificent , and we want to experience this exhilarating moment as well. Paris also offers breathtaking lights strung all around the city. A trip on their boats at night is definitely on our vacation schedule. Equally importantly, the food of Paris is undeniably mouth watering. The French are known for for its remarkable deserts, they are so fancy you don't want to ruin them by eating it! Even though it is ideal to visit Paris with your soul mate, visiting with friends is just as enjoyable overall.
